Stitch Up

4 player, 200pt extended, 2 ships exactly.

1st Player determined randomly then player order is clockwise from 1st Player. Alternatively, the player order can be determined randomly, but seating must go clockwise.

Ship spawn range 1 from edge, but within range 3 from your corner in either direction (spawn area is an "L" shape).

Each player brings 2 obstacles, but they are placed in player order until 6 are on the table.

Ships are placed, move and shoot in order of initiative and player order.

Winner is last pilot standing (or if called early, most points left on the table).

Alliances forbidden, but swaying your opponents choice of targets encouraged. If you have a shot, you must take it.
